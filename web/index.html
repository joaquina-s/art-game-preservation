keep the work running

Art games die when their engines go unmaintained and their servers shut down. revenant makes a self-contained copy that keeps running, and reads exactly what each work is built on. Where a piece of its world is already gone, the AI works as memory: it rebuilds what died, inside the limits the artist sets.
